"7 Favors" 2023 Cabernet Sauvignon

Overview

  • Appellation: North Coast - Lake County
  • Vineyard: Mount St. Helena Vineyard
  • Composition: 100% Cabernet Sauvignon (Primarily Clone 7, complemented by Clone 8)
  • Certification: Made with Organically Grown Grapes

Tasting Notes

Appearance: Deep ruby with garnet highlights, exuding vibrancy and depth.

Nose: An enticing bouquet of dark plum, cassis, and mocha with subtle black pepper and clove nuances. Meaty undertones with notes of cocoa, tobacco, and graphite.

Palate: Bold and refined with layers of black cherry, cassis, and forest berries. Savory umami notes enhance depth, supported by vibrant acidity and polished tannins.

Finish: Long and elegant, with lingering flavors of dark chocolate, espresso, and a mineral-savory edge.

Accolades

The 2022 edition earned 94 points from Wine Enthusiast Magazine, highlighting the exceptional quality of Mount St. Helena Vineyard’s organically grown fruit.

Pairing Suggestions

  • Roasted lamb
  • Braised short ribs
  • Wild mushroom risotto
  • Charcuterie or aged cheeses like Gruyère and Manchego

Additional Details

  • Aging: 12 months in French (10% American) oak barrels
  • Best From: 2025-2032
  • Analysis: TA: 6.36g/L, PH: 3.65, ALC: 14.5%
